Olla de Carne

For a comforting and hearty meal, Olla de Carne is the ultimate choice. This slow-cooked beef stew is packed with tender meat, yuca, plantains, corn, potatoes, and a variety of fresh vegetables. Rich in flavor and warmth, it’s the perfect dish to enjoy with family on a lazy Sunday afternoon. One bite and you’ll understand why this dish has been a staple in Costa Rican households for generations!

Chifrijo

Chifrijo is a delicious layered bowl of crispy chicharrón (fried pork), rice, beans, fresh pico de gallo, and crunchy tortilla chips. This dish is a favorite among locals looking for something flavorful, fun, and satisfying. Whether you’re enjoying it at a local restaurant or making it at home, Chifrijo is pure Tico goodness in every bite!
