places to be in Costa Rica!

A tiny peak of Costa Rica! Costa Rica’s vibrant spirit isn’t just in its cuisine—it’s woven into every corner of the country! These three iconic destinations capture the heart and soul of Costa Rican adventure. Get ready to explore the beauty of pura vida!

La Fortuna

Nestled at the base of the Arenal Volcano, La Fortuna is a charming town known for its lush rainforest, natural hot springs, and adventure tourism. Whether you're ziplining through the canopy, relaxing in a geothermal spring, or hiking to the stunning La Fortuna Waterfall, this destination offers a perfect mix of thrill and tranquility for travelers of all kinds.

manuel_antonio_national_park

Manuel Antonio National Park is a tropical paradise on Costa Rica's Pacific coast, famous for its white-sand beaches, hiking trails, and rich biodiversity. Visitors often spot monkeys, sloths, and exotic birds while exploring the park's scenic paths. The crystal-clear waters and nearby coral reefs make it a perfect spot for swimming and snorkeling.

volcan Arenal

Volcán Arenal is one of Costa Rica’s most iconic landmarks—a majestic volcano surrounded by dense forest and sprawling national parkland. Though now in a resting phase, it remains a popular spot for eco-tourism. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views, trek through lava fields, or relax in nearby hot springs fed by geothermal activity.
